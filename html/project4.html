About Me

Hello! My name is Ernesto, and I am currently a student and field technician. I enjoy my job and learning more about computer networking.

Outside of school, I like spending time on hobbies that keep me engaged and motivated. Whether it's working with computers, going to the gym, or learning something new, I always try to improve my skills.

My goal is to build a strong foundation in IT and Networking and pursue a career in it, where I can continue growing and solving real-world problems.

I currently work full time as a Field Technician with NCR, working on POS systems, Back office equipment and Networking Equipment for retailers such as Walmart and Home Depot

My job has been an important stepping stone into my career and has introduced me to many people and subjects that have helped me decide on what I want to do in the future.

Beginning college, I had no clue what I wanted to pursue in IT. I originally thought I would want to be a Software Developer, however with time and real-world experiences I began to explore networking as a career option

Over the course of the semester I have completed my CompTIA Network+ and have began studying for my CCNA to improve on my networking skills
